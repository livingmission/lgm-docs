# Introduction to Living Mission

Living Mission (LGM) is a next-generation **Delegated Proof-of-Stake (DPoS)** blockchain platform designed for high performance, security, and decentralization.

## What is Living Mission?

Living Mission is a Layer 1 blockchain that combines:

- **Fast Transactions**: ~1 second block times
- **Low Fees**: Efficient consensus minimizes costs
- **Decentralization**: Up to 101 validators
- **Staking Rewards**: Earn by securing the network
- **EVM Compatibility**: Deploy Solidity smart contracts

## Why Living Mission?

### For Users

- **Stake and Earn**: Delegate your LGM to validators and earn rewards
- **Low Barrier**: Start staking with just 1,000 LGM
- **Transparent**: All operations on-chain and verifiable
- **Secure**: Battle-tested smart contracts

### For Validators

- **Earn Rewards**: Block rewards + commission from delegators
- **Build Community**: Grow your delegation through referrals
- **Governance**: Participate in network decisions
- **Professional**: Enterprise-grade infrastructure

### For Developers

- **EVM Compatible**: Use familiar tools (Solidity, ethers.js, Hardhat)
- **Fast Finality**: Quick transaction confirmations
- **Rich APIs**: Comprehensive SDK and REST API
- **Documentation**: Extensive guides and references

## Key Innovations

### Resonance Consensus

Our custom DPoS consensus engine provides:

- **101 Validators**: More decentralized than most DPoS chains
- **1 Second Blocks**: Fast transaction processing
- **Epoch-Based Rewards**: Fair distribution every 200 blocks
- **System Transactions**: Automated reward distribution

### Proximity Rewards

A unique 8-level reward distribution system:

- Early stakers receive rewards from later stakers
- Incentivizes early participation
- Creates network effects for validators
- 87% distributed to uplines, 13% to claimer

### NFT Passport System

Controlled delegation through whitelisting:

- Validators control who can delegate
- Referral keys for community growth
- Anti-spam protection
- Relationship tracking

### Deflationary Tokenomics

Built-in burn mechanism:

- 10% tax on reward claims
- 9% burned, 1% to development
- Reduces supply over time
- Sustainable economics
